Our Mission

We guide SOF veterans from life’s storms toward peace through therapeutic sailing retreats. On calm seas, they reconnect with purpose, tribe, and hope.

The Retreat Experience

  • Seamanship & navigation
  • Daily meditation & yoga
  • Peer connection & shared meals
  • 3–5 day trips aboard a 50' yacht
  • 4–6 veterans per voyage

Why It Matters

Special operations veterans face unique post-service challenges. Helm & Haven offers a non-clinical path to wellness through purpose, movement, and brotherhood.
