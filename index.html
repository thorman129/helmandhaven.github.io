Our Mission

We guide SOF veterans away from life’s storms and toward calm waters through therapeutic sailing retreats. Each voyage is a course to peace, purpose, and community.

The Retreat Experience

  • Seamanship training
  • Daily yoga & mindfulness
  • Shared meals and stories
  • 3–5 day retreats
  • 50' sailing yacht | 4–6 participants per trip

Why It Matters

Special operations veterans face intense psychological tolls after service. Helm & Haven reconnects them to purpose, tribe, and nature in a way traditional therapy often cannot.
